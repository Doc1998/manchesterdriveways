How much does a new driveway cost in Altrincham?

Costs vary by surface type and size. Tarmac starts from around £30–50/m², block paving from £50–120/m², resin bound from £40–80/m², and pattern imprinted concrete from £50–90/m². A typical 50m² driveway in Altrincham costs approximately £1,500–£6,000. We provide free, detailed written quotes for all Altrincham and Trafford properties.

Do I need planning permission for a driveway in Altrincham?

Most driveways in Altrincham don't need planning permission if the surface is permeable or water drains to a lawn or soakaway rather than the road. Resin bound and permeable block paving are fully SuDS-compliant so never require permission. For non-permeable surfaces over 5m², you need to direct drainage appropriately. We advise on this at the free site survey.

What's the best driveway surface for Altrincham homes?

It depends on your property and priorities. Block paving — particularly tumbled clay with feature circles — suits the larger detached homes in Hale and Bowdon beautifully. Resin bound is ideal for a modern, low-maintenance finish and works well across Sale and Timperley. Tarmac is the most cost-effective. We'll always recommend what genuinely suits your property at the free survey.

How long does driveway installation take in Altrincham?

Tarmac typically takes 1–2 days. Resin bound takes 1–3 days. Block paving takes 2–5 days depending on size, pattern and whether feature circles are involved. Pattern imprinted concrete takes 2–3 days plus curing time. We agree a programme before starting and communicate clearly throughout.

Yes — all our driveway installations in Altrincham and across Trafford come with a 10-year workmanship guarantee. We've been in business since 1994 and stand fully behind our work. If anything fails due to workmanship in that period, we return and fix it at no cost to you.

Can you repair my existing driveway in Altrincham?

Yes. We carry out block paving repairs, repointing, relay and resin overlay work across all of Altrincham and Trafford. At the free site survey we'll assess the existing surface and give you an honest view on whether repair or full replacement is the better investment for your property.
