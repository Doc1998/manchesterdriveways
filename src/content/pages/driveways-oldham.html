Driveway Surfaces Available in Oldham

We install four types of driveway surface across Oldham and the surrounding area. Not sure which is right for you? We'll advise at the free site survey.

01

Block Paving

The most popular driveway surface in Oldham — hard-wearing, fully repairable, and available in dozens of colours and patterns including herringbone, stretcher bond and feature circles. Individual blocks can be replaced without disturbing the whole surface, making repairs simple and cost-effective for the life of the driveway.

From £50/m²
Lifespan 20–25yr
Install time 2–4 days

02

Resin Bound

Resin bound driveways are fully permeable, meaning surface water drains through the surface rather than running off — usually satisfying planning requirements without any application. The smooth, stone-aggregate finish looks sleek on modern Oldham properties, and with no loose stones or weeds to contend with, it's genuinely low-maintenance.

From £40/m²
Lifespan 15–25yr
Install time 1–2 days
Resin Driveways Oldham
03

Tarmac Driveways

Tarmac is the most cost-effective driveway surface available and can be installed faster than any other option — often in a single day. Modern tarmac driveways can be finished with a range of edging treatments and decorative borders, and when laid on a correctly specified sub-base they provide a durable, low-maintenance surface for 15–20 years.

From £30/m²
Lifespan 15–20yr
Install time 1–2 days
Tarmac Driveways Oldham
04

Pattern Imprinted Concrete

Pattern imprinted concrete — sometimes called stamped concrete or decorative concrete — offers the look of block paving, cobblestones or natural stone at a lower price point. Coloured and patterned before it cures, it creates a seamless, low-joint surface that's popular in Oldham for both driveways and patios. We seal all our PIC installations for long-term colour retention.

From £50/m²
Lifespan 20–25yr
Install time 2–3 days

Driveways Oldham — Common Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most often from Oldham homeowners.

How much does a new driveway cost in Oldham?

Costs depend on surface type and size. Tarmac starts from around £30–£50/m², resin bound from £40–£80/m², block paving from £50–£120/m², and pattern imprinted concrete from £50–£90/m². A typical 40m² driveway would cost £1,200–£4,800 depending on material. We provide free, itemised quotes for all Oldham properties with no obligation.

Do I need planning permission for a driveway in Oldham?

Most driveways in Oldham don't require planning permission. Permeable surfaces — including resin bound and permeable block paving — generally require no permission regardless of size, as surface water drains naturally through them. For non-permeable surfaces over 5m², water must drain to a lawn, border or soakaway rather than the road. We'll advise on the right approach at the free site survey.

How long does driveway installation take in Oldham?

Tarmac driveways typically take 1–2 days. Resin bound driveways take 1–2 days. Block paving takes 2–4 days depending on size and pattern complexity. Pattern imprinted concrete takes 2–3 days plus curing time before the driveway can be driven on. We'll give you a clear confirmed programme with your written quote.

Which driveway surface is best for an Oldham home?

It depends on your property, budget and priorities. Block paving suits period properties, is fully repairable and lasts 20–25 years. Resin bound looks modern, is fully permeable and genuinely low-maintenance. Tarmac is the most affordable and quickest to install. Pattern imprinted concrete gives a decorative finish at a mid-range price point. We'll recommend the right option for your specific Oldham property at the free site survey.

Do you offer a guarantee on driveways in Oldham?

Yes — all our driveway installations in Oldham come with a 10-year workmanship guarantee. If the surface fails due to workmanship within 10 years, we'll return and fix it at no cost to you. The guarantee is provided in writing with every job.

Can you remove and replace my existing driveway in Oldham?

Yes. We handle the full process — breaking out and removing the existing surface, disposing of all waste off-site, building the correct sub-base, and installing the new surface. We also carry out partial repairs and relay of existing driveways across Oldham where replacement isn't necessary. We'll always give you an honest view on whether repair or replacement is the better option.
