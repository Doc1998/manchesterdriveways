Why Clean your roof? A dirty roof, particularly one with moss or algae growth, can trap moisture over time. This excess moisture can infiltrate the fascia, soffit, and roof structure, leading to rot and deterioration. As these components weaken, the gutters attached to them may also suffer damage, potentially compromising the overall integrity of your roof system.s moisture exposure can lead to rot, deterioration, and damage to these components, ultimately affecting the gutters that are attached to them.
