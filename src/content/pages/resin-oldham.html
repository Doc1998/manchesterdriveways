Resin Bound vs Resin Bonded

Two different products, both using resin — but with very different results. Here's what you need to know before choosing for your Oldham property.

Resin Bound

Most Popular

Aggregate stones are fully mixed with UV-stable resin before being laid to create a smooth, porous, seamless surface. The aggregate is locked into the resin — there are no loose stones. Fully permeable and SuDS-compliant.

This is the premium product and accounts for the majority of our Oldham resin installations. The finish is refined and modern, and it lasts 15–25 years with a correctly prepared base.

  • AppearanceSmooth, seamless
  • Loose stones?✓ None
  • Permeable✓ Fully
  • Planning permission✓ Not required
  • Typical cost£40–£70/m²
  • Lifespan15–25 years

Resin Bonded

Budget Option

Resin is applied to the existing surface as a bonding coat, then aggregate is scattered on top and excess swept away. The aggregate sits proud of the resin giving a textured, stone-chip appearance. Not permeable.

A good lower-cost option where drainage isn't the primary concern, or where the existing surface is in good condition. Can lose a small number of stones over time.

  • AppearanceTextured, stone chip
  • Loose stones?Limited initially
  • Permeable✗ Not permeable
  • Planning permissionMay be required (5m²+)
  • Typical cost£20–£35/m²
  • Lifespan8–15 years

How We Install Resin Driveways in Oldham

Every resin installation follows the same five-stage process — refined over 30 years to deliver consistent, long-lasting results.

1

Free Survey

We visit your Oldham property, assess the existing surface, soil conditions, and drainage. We bring aggregate samples so you can choose your colour in natural light.

2

Prep & Base

If needed: excavation to required depth, installation of MOT Type 1 sub-base and compaction. If laying over existing concrete/tarmac, we assess, repair, and prime the surface.

3

Macadam Base

Open-graded porous asphalt (macadam) is laid to create a stable, permeable base for the resin layer. Edging restraints installed to permanent specification.

4

Resin & Aggregate

UV-stable resin is mixed with your chosen aggregate and hand-trowelled to an even 15–18mm depth. The surface is screeded perfectly level. Typically sets within 6–8 hours.

5

Clean & Handover

All waste removed, site fully cleaned, and a complete walkthrough of aftercare advice and your 10-year guarantee documentation before we leave your property.

Resin Driveway FAQ — Oldham

Common questions from Oldham homeowners about resin bound and resin bonded driveways.

How much does a resin driveway cost in Oldham?

Resin bound driveways in Oldham typically cost £40–£70 per square metre. A standard 40m² driveway costs approximately £1,600–£2,800. Resin bonded is cheaper at £20–£35/m². Cost varies based on prep work required, aggregate choice, and whether we're laying over an existing base. We provide free, detailed written quotes.

What's the difference between resin bound and resin bonded?

Resin bound mixes aggregate fully into resin before laying — smooth, permeable, no loose stones, no planning permission needed. Resin bonded applies resin to the surface then scatters aggregate on top — textured appearance, not permeable, lower cost. We install both and will recommend the right one at your free survey.

Do I need planning permission for a resin driveway in Oldham?

Resin bound driveways are permeable so they satisfy SuDS requirements — no planning permission needed regardless of size. This is one of resin bound's biggest advantages over non-permeable surfaces like resin bonded, block paving or tarmac where surfaces over 5m² do require permission (or a permeable build-up).

How long does resin last in Oldham's climate?

A correctly installed resin bound driveway lasts 15–25 years. We use UV-stable resins that resist yellowing and colour fade — important for Oldham's variable weather. Frost resistance is built in through the flexible resin formulation and the permeable build-up (no pooling water to freeze and crack the surface). We back all Oldham resin installations with a 10-year guarantee.

Can resin be laid over my existing tarmac or concrete?

Often yes — if your existing tarmac or concrete is structurally sound, free of major cracking, and well-drained, we can install resin bound directly on top. We assess this at the free site survey. Laying over an existing base saves excavation cost and reduces installation time significantly.

How long does resin driveway installation take in Oldham?

A standard resin driveway in Oldham takes 1–2 days if laying over an existing base. If full excavation and new sub-base is required, allow 2–3 days. The resin itself sets in 6–8 hours under normal conditions — we advise light pedestrian use after 24 hours and vehicle use after 48 hours.
