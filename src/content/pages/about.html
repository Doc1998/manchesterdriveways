Why Choose Us

What Makes Us Different

A lot of driveway companies say the same things. Here's what we actually do differently — and why it matters for your driveway.

01

We Never Skimp on the Sub-base

The sub-base is what your driveway is built on. Lay it too shallow, use the wrong material, or skip proper compaction — and the surface above it will fail. We use compacted MOT Type 1 on every single job, assessed and specified to the correct depth for your ground conditions. It's more work. It costs more. And it's why our driveways last 20+ years.

02

We're Actually Local

We're not a national lead-generation service that subcontracts your job to whoever's available. We are the people who will lay your driveway. We're based in Greater Manchester, we know the local ground conditions, we know the planning requirements, and when something needs sorting after the job is done, you'll be speaking to the same team who did the work.

03

A Real 10-Year Guarantee

Our 10-year workmanship guarantee isn't small print. It's a commitment we make because we're confident in how we build. If a surface fails due to workmanship within 10 years, we'll fix it. We've been in business for over 30 years — we're not going anywhere, and neither are our guarantees.

04

Honest Advice, Not a Hard Sell

We'll visit your property for free, assess what's actually there, and tell you honestly what we think is the right approach — even if that means recommending a repair rather than a full replacement. We'd rather lose a sale than put in work that isn't right for your property. That's how you build the kind of reputation that lasts 30 years.

How We Work

The principles that have guided every job since 1994.

Quality Materials Only

We specify MOT Type 1 sub-base, quality block manufacturers, and UV-stable resins on every job. No substitutions to cut costs mid-project.

We Show Up on Time

We agree a start date and we're there. We know how disruptive driveway work is — we minimise that disruption by communicating clearly and keeping to schedule.

Transparent Pricing

Our quotes are itemised and in writing. No verbal estimates, no surprise additions. The price we quote is the price you pay, unless you change the spec.

We Clean Up Properly

All excavation waste removed, surrounding areas swept and reinstated, and a full site clean before we leave. Your neighbours will barely know we were there.

Aftercare & Support

After your driveway is complete, we're still available. We walk you through maintenance advice and answer any questions in the weeks after installation.

Built on Referrals

The majority of our work comes from customer referrals and repeat business. That's the best quality guarantee we can offer — people trust us enough to recommend us to their neighbours.
