Do I need planning permission for a driveway in Stockport?

In most cases, you do not need planning permission for a new driveway in Stockport if the surface is permeable (such as resin bound or gravel). If you use an impermeable surface like standard block paving or concrete, you will need to ensure that rainwater drains to a lawn, border, or soakaway rather than onto the public highway. Our team will advise you on the most appropriate surface for your property and planning situation.

How long does a driveway installation take in Stockport?

Most driveway installations in Stockport take between one and five days, depending on the size and material. A standard block paving driveway typically takes two to three days, while a resin bound driveway can often be completed in a single day. Tarmac driveways are also very fast to install. We'll give you a clear timeline when we provide your quote.

Is block paving or resin better for a Stockport driveway?

Both are excellent choices, and the best option depends on your priorities. Block paving offers a traditional look with easy individual block repairs, and is widely used across Stockport. Resin bound is fully permeable, extremely low maintenance, and gives a sleek modern appearance — it's also ideal for meeting planning requirements. We can show you examples of both during your free site visit and help you choose the right surface for your home.

How long will my new Stockport driveway last?

When properly installed and maintained, most driveway surfaces last between 15 and 25 years. Block paving and pattern imprinted concrete typically last 20–25 years, resin bound 15–20 years, and tarmac 15–20 years with periodic sealing.
