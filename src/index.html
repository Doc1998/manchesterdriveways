What should I look for in a driveway contractor?

  • Experience & Reputation: Choose a contractor with a solid track record in driveway installations and repairs.
  • Fully Insured: Ensure the contractor carries public liability insurance to protect your property.
  • Detailed Written Quotes: Obtain itemised quotes so you know exactly what's included — no hidden costs.
  • References & Reviews: Check Google reviews and ask for photos of completed jobs to gauge quality.
  • Material Guarantees: A reputable contractor will back their work with a written workmanship guarantee.

What driveway materials are best for UK homes?

  • Block Paving: Versatile, attractive, and easy to repair — one of the most popular choices in the UK.
  • Resin Bound: A smooth, permeable finish that meets SuDS regulations and requires minimal maintenance.
  • Tarmac / Asphalt: A cost-effective, durable surface ideal for larger driveways and quick installation.
  • Pattern Imprinted Concrete: Mimics the look of stone or brick at a lower cost, with a long lifespan.
  • Gravel: An affordable, permeable option that's easy to install and acts as a natural deterrent.
